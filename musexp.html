Musical Experience

STRYKE Percusson Ensemble

In 2011 I joined Stryke Percussion Ensemble in Palm Beach, Florida. That year we placed 2nd in the Winter Guard International Open Class Championships. In 2012 we were moved up to World Class and made it into finals. When I moved up to Gainesville, I left the ensemble. In 2015 I returned to take the position of center marimba and we placed 8th in World Finals and were voted Fan Favorite. To the right is a video from 2015.

Carolina Crown

In the Summer of 2013, I joined the Drum Corp International Ensemble, Carolina Crown. That summer I played marimba and traveled around the United States playing for many crowds. Our ensemble won the gold medal in world championships. To the left is our encore run after finals.
